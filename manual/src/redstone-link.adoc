= Redstone Link

Redstone links enable remote transmission and reception of redstone signals, as described in its in-game ponder. When placed in factory controllers, they may be connected with other components with redstone capabilities, allowing them to interoperate with contraptions outside the factory controller.

== Operating Principle

A redstone link has 2 modes of operation:

[horizontal]
Send:: Sends the powered state of its incoming redstone connections to other redstone links of the same channel.
Receive:: Receives from other redstone links of the same channel, if any of them is powered, powers its outgoing redstone connections.

Redstone links are distinguished with channels, which consists of 2 item types. Each channel carries a redstone signal independent from each other. Examples of distinct channels are:

- (Oak Plank, Cobblestone)
- (Oak Plank, Air)
- (Air, Oak Plank)
- (Air, Air)

== Configuration

After placing a redstone link on the virtual board, clicking the component opens its configuration screen.

.Redstone link settings
image::redstone-link-settings.png[,384,207]

&#x2776; &#x2777; Channel::
  Place item, or drag ghost item from JEI to set the channel of the redstone link.
&#x2778; Relocate button::
  Click to move the location of this redstone link on the virtual board.
&#x2779; Add connection button::
  Click to connect to other components via redstone. Directions of connections are decided automatically. Send-mode links may only have incoming connections, receive-mode links may only have outgoing connections.
&#x277A; Mode switch::
  Click to switch between send image:btn-send-mode.png[,18,18] and receive image:btn-receive-mode.png[,18,18] mode.
&#x277B; Confirm button::
  Click to confirm settings and close the screen.
